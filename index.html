Scoundrels

Health: 20/20
Weapon: None
Last Fought: 0
Cards Left: 0

Game Rules

Goal: Clear the deck and the room without dying.

Health: You start with 20 HP. If it drops to 0, you lose.

Cards

  • ♥ Hearts (Potions): Heal HP equal to value.
  • ♦ Diamonds (Weapons): Equip a weapon. Value is attack power.
  • ♠ Spades / ♣ Clubs (Monsters): Fight them!

Combat

Damage taken = Monster Value.

With Weapon: Damage = max(0, Monster Value - Weapon Value).

Restriction: You cannot use a weapon if the monster is stronger than or equal to the last monster defeated by that weapon.

Actions

Run: You can skip a room once per turn cycle. Cards go to the bottom of the deck. You cannot skip two rooms in a row.
